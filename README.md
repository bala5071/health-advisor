Health Advisor is a mobile app that helps people make safer food and product decisions in seconds. Scan a label with your camera and receive a personalized health verdict with clear, actionable guidance. Built for real-world use, it combines speed, privacy, and medically relevant insights in one experience.

## What is Health Advisor?

People managing chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, or food allergies often struggle to quickly judge whether a product is safe for them. Health Advisor solves this by turning any phone camera into a real-time health decision assistant. After a quick scan, the app evaluates the product against your personal health profile and returns an instant verdict: **APPROVED**, **CAUTION**, or **AVOID**. The result is practical, personalized guidance you can trust while shopping.

## Key Features

- 📷 Instant product scanning via camera
- 🤖 On-device AI — no data leaves your phone
- 🩺 Personalized for your conditions, medications, and allergies
- ⚠️ Allergen detection with severity classification
- 📊 Nutrition analysis with FDA threshold flagging
- 📈 Weekly and monthly health reports
- 🔒 Privacy-first — all health data stored locally
- 🗣️ Voice readout of scan results
- 🚨 Emergency health info screen

## How It Works

1. Set up your health profile — add conditions, medications, allergies.
2. Point your camera at any product label.
3. AI analyzes the product in seconds.
4. Get an instant verdict: **APPROVED**, **CAUTION**, or **AVOID**.
5. View detailed nutrition facts, allergen warnings, and personalized explanation.
6. Track your health trends over time with weekly reports.

## Privacy & Security

- All health data is stored on-device only.
- No health information is sent to any server.
- AI inference runs on-device with no cloud processing.
- GDPR-compliant data export and deletion is supported.

## Tech Stack

React Native · Expo SDK 55 · On-device AI (llama.rn) · WatermelonDB · Supabase Auth
